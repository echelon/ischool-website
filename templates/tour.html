About the Tour

Join the iSchool Initiative on our ambitious mission to reform education throughout the United States. The Digital Learning Revolution Tour is an educational conference on wheels. This tour is the culmination of what we preach as mobile learners, as it gives us the ability to extend our reach beyond our back yard and affect the lives of students across the country. For six months, iSchool Initiative will travel to educational conferences, schools, and communities to bring excitement around mobile learning and showcase other engaging technology made for the classroom.

Documentary

iSchool Initiative will be filming a documentary throughout our tour featuring the school districts and teachers we will be working with. We will be filming many of the presentations and posting them online for the schools we visit to access as well.

"The documentary will tell the compelling story of the iSchool Initiative's tour while providing an overview of the Digital Learning Revolution. It will also demonstrate the stark contrast between the traditional, outdated forms of classroom learning and the highly-engaging and effective approaches afforded by the iPad." - Joseph East

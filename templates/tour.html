Leading the Digital Learning Revolution

iSchool Initiative's grassroots movement to reform education takes to the road for its Second Annual Digital Learning Revolution (DLR) Tour. The DLR Tour is the culmination of our message to mobile learners and gives us the ability to affect the lives of students across the country. iSchool Initiative travels to a variety of schools, conferences, and communities to showcase its exciting vision of what a 21st century mobile learning classroom can be. Join us this year on a six-month long journey across the United States, which truly represents the “Mobile Classroom Experience.”

The Mobile Classroom Experience

For the 2013 DLR Tour, iSchool Initiative is excited to announce the introduction of the Mobile Classroom Experience: a 38-foot bus outfitted with a complete mobile solution for schools. Paired with inspiring keynote presentations and workshops, this classroom-on-wheels demonstrates exactly how mobile technology integration can be accomplished. We have merged services from nine different companies into the mobile space with the purpose of giving students and educators the full experience of their potential future classroom. Inside, you‘ll see how iSchool Initiative has unified an industry to make change an attainable reality.

Schedule a Tour Stop

iSchool Initiative DLR Tour stops are an opportunity to enhance the knowledge and participation of your teachers, students, and community. Customize this conference on wheels to your specific needs to pursue your mobile dream.

Our sponsors aren‘t just sponsors,
they‘re partners in the cause.

With its roots in education, Promethean has been empowering teachers for over 15 years. This past March, we joined forces with CEO Jim Marshall, SVP of Marketing Lupita Knittel, and other leaders to create an ambitious partnership. Promethean strongly believes that learning never ends. It was this love of lifelong learning, alongside their work to create connected learning systems with mobile devices and other classroom technology, that convinced us we have found a great match. We are grateful to have the experience, and dedicated support of Promethean throughout the 2013 DLR Tour.

Otterbox is a purveyor of high-quality, durable cases for mobile devices. iSchool Initiative first collaborated with Otterbox during its 2012 DLR Tour. Stopping by the Otterbox headquarters in beautiful Fort Collins, Colorado, our team discovered a company that is truly dedicated to empowering youth and giving back to the community that fostered its growth. Working with Nancy Richardson (wife of founder Kurt Richardson) and her team at OtterCares, iSchool Initiative began a partnership tasked with protecting the mobile device investments schools are making across the country.

Why We’re Here

iSchool Initiative is a young start-up with huge aspirations! Our goal is to revolutionize the education system through a grassroots movement advocating for contemporary technology integration. We motivate both students and teachers to become lifelong digital learners by inspiring action, providing comprehensive training on the use of technology, and guiding educational institutions through technology deployment.

Our Journey

On a spring day in 2009, a high school student named Travis Allen decided to use his smartphone to take class notes. Assuming it was a distraction and serving no purpose, his teacher promptly confiscated the device and disciplined him. Frustrated, Travis realized the difficulty stemmed from a gap between the school experience and the tools available to students. From that day forward, he has dedicated his life to mobile learning and changing education to suit today’s youth. Later that year, Travis formed the iSchool Initiative with several other students at Kennesaw State University in Georgia. The growth of the organization was fostered through the collegiate group Enactus (formerly known as SIFE), and under the mentorship of world class professors such as Dr. Gary Roberts. The team is now comprised of 13 ambitious students with a diverse background of skills and experiences. They continue to balance their studies with a life on the road, and are dedicated to making education relevant in students’ lives again.

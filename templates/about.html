Organically founded by Travis Allen in late 2009, iSchool Initiative was formed to address the educational issues facing students nationwide.

Since its inception, the iSchool Initiative team has expanded to 11 full-time team members and made appearances at many prestigious events worldwide, including a recent invitation to a White House Data Initiative. iSchool Initiative continues to support the movement for change in education as a leader of the Digital Learning Revolution.

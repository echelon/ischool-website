Why We're Here

iSchool Initiative is a young start-up with huge aspirations! Our goal is to revolutionize the education system through a grassroots movement advocating for contemporary technology integration. Our team motivates both students and teachers to become lifelong digital learners by inspiring action, providing comprehensive training on the use of technology, and guiding educational institutions through technology deployment.

Our Journey

On a spring day in 2009, a high school student named Travis Allen decided to use his smartphone in class to supplement his learning. His teacher quickly determined that Travis was up to no good and took up the cellphone, assuming the device was a distraction in the classroom as opposed to a useful tool. From that day forward, Travis has dedicated his life to mobile learning and began by creating a YouTube video that went viral and led to the creation of iSchool Initiative. Once Travis entered college at Kennesaw State University, he fostered the growth of the organization through the Enactus student group, and under the mentorship and guidance of world class professors such as Dr. Gary Roberts, iSchool Initiative now sets the standard for student-led Professional Learning.
